Mission

K.K.Nagar A.G. Church exists to glorify God by bringing Salvation, Healing, Freedom and Blessing to the City of Trichy, Surrounding Villages, the District of Trichy, the State of Tamilnadu, the Nation of India and all the Nations of the World, through prayer, preaching, teaching, healing and demonstration of the Word of God, the Bible.
